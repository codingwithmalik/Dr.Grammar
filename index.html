Introduction to Grammar and Composition

Writing helps us share our thoughts, tell stories, and explain ideas. To become great writers, we need to understand two important things: grammar and composition.

What is Grammar?

Grammar is the set of rules that helps us write and speak correctly. It teaches us how to build sentences and use the right words. Using good grammar makes our writing easier to read and understand.

  • When to use capital letters
  • How to use punctuation (like periods and commas)
  • What nouns, verbs, and adjectives are

What is Composition?

Composition means putting our ideas together in a clear and organized way. It helps us write stories, letters, or reports that others enjoy reading. Persuasive writing tries to make the reader agree with you.

  • Narrative – tells a story
  • Descriptive – describes a person or place
  • Expository – explains something
  • Persuasive – tries to make the reader agree with you

Why It Matters?

Grammar and composition work together to make your writing strong, clear, and fun to read. The more you practice, the better you'll get!
